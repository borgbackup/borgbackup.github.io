Borg 1.0

The old stable series is Borg 1.0, with the initial 1.0.0 release on 5th March 2016. The latest release in this series is Borg 1.0.11, released on 21st July 2017. This is the series shipped by most Linux distributions.

Borg 0.xx

Borg 0.xx was the development series leading up to the Borg 1.0 release and is not supported any more. It was active from early 2015 to early 2016 including releases from 0.23.0 to 0.30.1.
